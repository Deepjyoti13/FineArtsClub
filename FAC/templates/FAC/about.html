Fine Arts Club

National · Institute · of · Technology · Agartala. · fineartsclub.nita@gmail.com

The Fine Arts Club was founded with an aim to bring out the creative bone of the students of college. It has been started to help students break free from monotonous college life and bring out their creativity. It is one of the most active club of the college. The club functions throughout the year in various events. It also conducts two exhibitions yearly as part of both the technical fest AAYAM and cultural fest MOKSHA.
